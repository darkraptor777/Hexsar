Instructions

Ship can be controlled with either wasd, the arrow keys or the left joystick.
Press/Hold space or the A button on a 360 controller to fire.
Enemies that spawn in groups have a chance to drop pickups when all of them are destroyed, 
the pickups are spawned after a delay at the center of the screen.
Enemies that are not part of groups have a chance to drop pickups when they are destroyed,
the pickups are spawned immediately at the location of destruction.
Pickups are additive, but are lost when you die.
The aim is to get as many points as you can and beat the boss
